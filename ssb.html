Major Wars & Operations

1947-48: First Kashmir War
Post-independence tribal invasion.
1962: Sino-Indian War
High-altitude conflict over Aksai Chin/NEFA borders. Sparked major Indian military modernization.
1965: Indo-Pakistani War
Response to Operation Gibraltar; massive tank battles.
1971: Bangladesh Liberation War
Decisive victory led by Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw.
1999: Kargil War (Op Vijay)
High-altitude warfare to evict intruders from peaks.
May 2025: Operation Sindoor
A highly critical modern case study: Launched in response to the Pahalgam terror attack. A massive, tri-service integrated campaign targeting LeT/JeM infrastructure deep in Pakistan and PoK.

The Tri-Service Ace: Theaterisation

The ongoing process to integrate the Army, Navy, and Air Force into unified "Theatre Commands" under single operational commanders, rather than fighting as separate entities. This is a highly probable interview topic.

  • Current Tri-Service Commands: Andaman and Nicobar Command (ANC) & Strategic Forces Command (SFC - Nuclear).
  • Chief of Defence Staff (CDS): The primary role of the CDS is to facilitate this integration and act as the principal military advisor to the Defence Minister.

The SSB Process & 15 OLQs

This is how you are evaluated. The entire 5-day process is designed to test your personality against the 15 Officer-Like Qualities.

The 5-Day Evaluation

Day 1: Screening (Stage I)

  • • OIR Test: Verbal & Non-Verbal reasoning.
  • • PPDT: Picture Perception & Description Test (Write a story, narrate it, and do a Group Discussion).
  • *Candidates who fail leave today.

Day 2: Psychological Tests

  • • TAT: Thematic Apperception Test (12 Pictures).
  • • WAT: Word Association Test (60 Words, 15s each).
  • • SRT: Situation Reaction Test (60 situations, 30 min).
  • • SDT: Self-Description Test.

Days 3 & 4: GTO Tasks

  • • Group Discussion (GD) & Group Planning Exercise (GPE).
  • • Progressive & Half Group Tasks (PGT/HGT).
  • • Snake Race (Group Obstacle Race).
  • • Individual Obstacles (IO) & Lecturette.
  • • Command Task (CT) & Final Group Task (FGT).

Day 5: The Conference

Final board meeting with all assessors. Results declared.

*Personal Interviews (PI) are scheduled in the afternoons of Days 2, 3, or 4.

Critical Defence Policies

1. Agnipath Scheme (Agniveers)

Recruitment of soldiers below commissioned officer rank for a 4-year tenure. Goal is a younger demographic profile and reduced pension bills. After 4 years, up to 25% are retained in regular cadre; the rest exit with a 'Seva Nidhi' package.

2. Nuclear Doctrine

Based on "No First Use" (NFU) and "Credible Minimum Deterrence". Nuclear weapons can only be authorized by the civilian political leadership through the Nuclear Command Authority (NCA).

3. Make in India (Atmanirbharta)

Focus on self-reliance in defence. Key initiatives: iDEX (Innovations for Defence Excellence), two Defence Industrial Corridors (UP & Tamil Nadu), and negative import lists.

CAPF & Defence Technology

Understanding the difference between Armed Forces and Paramilitary, and knowing the PSUs that build India's arsenal.

Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF)

Under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), not MoD.

  • Border Security Force (BSF): Guards Indo-Pak & Indo-Bangladesh borders.
  • CRPF: Largest force; handles internal security, riots, and Naxal operations.
  • ITBP: Guards the high-altitude Indo-China border.
  • Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB): Guards Indo-Nepal & Indo-Bhutan borders.
  • CISF: Secures vital installations (airports, nuclear plants, metro).
  • Assam Rifles: Oldest paramilitary; guards Indo-Myanmar border.

Key Defence Organizations

  • DRDO: R&D agency. Developers of Agni, Prithvi, Akash, Nag missiles, and Tejas LCA.
  • HAL (Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd): Manufactures Tejas, ALH Dhruv, LCH Prachand, Sukhoi-30MKI.
  • BEL (Bharat Electronics Ltd): Electronics, radars, electronic warfare systems.
  • BDL (Bharat Dynamics Ltd): Manufactures missiles and underwater weapons.
  • Ordnance Factory Board (Now 7 PSUs): Manufactures arms, ammunition, and artillery.
